Welcome to Liit's Special Baye-Baye, a cherished family owned business founded in 1991. Baye-Baye is a famously known delicacy from the town of Pavia in Iloilo, Philippines. Our Baye-Baye is a symphony of flavors, skillfully blending fresh young coconut, toasted pinipig (pounded young rice grains), and sweetness from sugar. What makes us apart is not just its delectable taste but the commitment to preserving the authentic preperation methods that have remained unchanged throughout the years.
