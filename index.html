Welcome to Liit's Special Baye-Baye, a cherished family-owned business founded in 1991. Baye-Baye is a famously known delicacy from the town of Pavia in Iloilo, Philippines. Our Baye-Baye is a symphony of flavors, skillfully blending fresh young coconut, toasted pinipig (pounded young rice grains), and sweetness from sugar. What sets us apart is our commitment to preserving the authentic preparation methods that have remained unchanged throughout the years.
